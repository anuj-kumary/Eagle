Text Utilities

Text Utilities are used to control alignments with different sizes and also to center text.

Heading
All HTML headings, <h1> through <h6>, are available.

H-1 Heading 1

H-2 Heading 2

H-3 Heading 3

H-4 Heading 4

H-5 Heading 5
H-6 Heading 6
                    
<h1>H-1 Heading 1</h1>
<h2>H-2 Heading 2</h2>
<h3>H-3 Heading 3</h3>
<h4>H-4 Heading 4</h4>
<h5>H-5 Heading 5</h5>
<h6>H-6 Heading 6</h6>
Alignment
Use classes .text__left , .text__center or .text__right to center, left or right align text.
Left Align
Center Align
Right Align 
                    
<h6 class="text__left">Left Align </h6>
<h6 class="text__center">Center Align </h6>
<h6 class="text__right">Right Align </h6>
font-size
Use classes .fs__1, .fs__2, .fs__3 , .fs__4, .fs__5 or .fs__6 to change the font-size of text.

Font Weight
Use classes .fw__100, .fw__300, .fw__400 , .fw__700 or .fw__900 to change the font-weight of text ranging from 100 to 900.

<h5 class="fw__300">Font weight 300</h5>
<h5 class="fw__100">Font weight 100</h5>
